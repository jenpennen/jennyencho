# About Me

## What I Do

I'm a ux/product designer currently working on internal tools at Develop For Good. Formally, I'm a design engineer - I code my designs.

## Why I Design & Code

I was drawn to design through my engineering projects at UCLA. I would constantly ask myself: how can I blend my technical skills with design to engineer authentic and nostalgic experiences in a digital world?

## My Manifesto

My engineering background plays an integral role in my design workflow and vice versa. Whether in my professional work or side projects, I strive to combine clean design principles with thoughtful engineering to make online interactions seamless, natural, and enjoyable.
